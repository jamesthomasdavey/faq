Giraffe Trivia

Fun facts about giraffes!

How tall can giraffes get?

Male giraffes can some times reach heights of around 18 feet! That's three times taller than your dad!

How fast are giraffes?

Over short distances, giraffes can run at speeds up to 35mph!

How quickly do baby giraffes learn to walk?

Baby giraffes can learn to stand up and even run within an hour of being born!
